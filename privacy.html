GearStack

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 29, 2025  ·  Last updated: May 29, 2025

GearStack is an internal business tool used by a single operator. It does not collect, store, or share any personal data from visitors or third parties.

Who We Are

GearStack is a camera reseller intelligence tool operated by Grainwashed, a sole-proprietor camera resale and repair business. The application is accessible at gearstack-app-pzf3.vercel.app.

Data We Collect

GearStack does not collect personal information from any user. There are no accounts, no registration forms, no analytics tracking, and no cookies placed on visitor devices.

Inventory and business data entered into the application is stored locally in the user's browser via localStorage and is never transmitted to any external server controlled by this application.

Third-Party Services

GearStack connects to the following external APIs in the course of normal operation:

eBay API — used to retrieve publicly available listing data for market research. No eBay user account data is accessed or stored. eBay's own privacy policy governs their data handling.

EstateSales.net — publicly available sale listing data is fetched for research purposes only. No user data from that platform is stored.

Anthropic Claude API — used for image analysis features. Images submitted for analysis are subject to Anthropic's privacy policy and data handling terms.

eBay Marketplace Account Deletion

GearStack does not store any eBay user account data, buyer information, or transaction records associated with eBay accounts. As a result, this application has no data to delete in response to eBay Marketplace Account Deletion notifications. This application is operated solely for internal business use and does not process end-user data from the eBay platform.

Data Retention

No personal data is collected, and therefore no personal data is retained. Business inventory data stored in localStorage remains on the operator's device and is under the operator's full control.

Children's Privacy

GearStack is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be directed to the operator via eBay messages at username Grainwashed.
